About_

Hello there!

I am a Computer Science graduate from Independent University, Bangladesh, deeply immersed in pioneering research at the intersection of Data Science and healthcare.

The research focus is mainly on Biomedical AI, aiming to serve the healthcare sector and the clinicians with assistive systems like computer-aided diagnosis (CADx), disease detection systems to ease treatment procedures. My expertise lies in leveraging modern deep learning techniques for automatic region of interest (ROI) localization from medical imaging, specifically focusing on Computer Tomography (CT) images.

Currently I'm a Research assistant in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Independent University, Bangladesh. Beyond research, I actively contributed to collaborative AI projects that focus on solving real-world problems. As a former executive committee member of the IEEE Computer Society IUB Student Branch Chapter, I've been deeply involved in fostering tech engagement. Additionally, I've represented my university in Hackathons, showcasing my commitment to innovation and teamwork.

With a passion for bridging industry and academia, my pursuit extends in multitude, showcasing a versatile approach to technology and innovation. Apart from this, I'm a football fanatic, an average gamer and love to engage in multitude of interests.
